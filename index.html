About the school

A focused week of learning, building and connecting with GeoAI

The summer school combines lectures, hands-on labs and research talks to take participants from core principles to the cutting edge advances of AI for Earth observation — with plenty of time to discuss, build and meet collaborators.

  • Lectures from leading researchers
  • Industry sessions
  • Hands-on labs & tutorials
  • Cutting-edge research talks
  • Networking
  • Practical, transferable skills
